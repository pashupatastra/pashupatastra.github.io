Forward3D is an international digital agency with offices in London, New York, Shanghai and Seoul. Forward3D has grown rapidly since it’s inception in 2004, and has since repositioned itself from a ‘techy’ digital startup to a successful high-end agency dealing with big name clients in the luxury, fashion and travel sectors. The rebrand was essential in this, as the old web 2.0 feel of the company’s visuals had become outdated and no longer represented Forward3D’s status and direction. The new logo and visual language has a fresher feel, more focused on the fashion industry and more appealing to it’s target market. View the Forward3D identity guidelines here.
