A personal project consisting of five silkscreen printed, square posters dedicated to the words of five influencial figures: Mark E. Smith, Mos Def, Bill Hicks, Tony Benn and John Peel. The prints focus on a quotation from each person, using imagery to represent their words and opacities of metallic ink to heighten their sense of value and highlight the respect I have towards them.

"Listen mate, life has surface noise." – John Peel
(responding to a claim that CDs are better than records because they "don't have surface noise")

"If you're gonna play it out of tune, play it out of tune properly" – Mark E. Smith

"Young bloods can't add but they can rock you at Playstation" – Mos Def

"Ideas are more powerful than guns" – Tony Benn

"Don't worry, don't be afraid ever, because this is just a ride" – Bill Hicks
